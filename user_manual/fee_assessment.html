In fee assessment the user prior taking an appointment can calculate the stamp duty and registration fee needed for registration by just selecting the deed category and sub deed category. Fee depends on the various deed category available, additionally in case of Sale or Gift deed category, a Consideration Amount along with some extra fields are needed to entered by the user.

After selecting the required deed and sub deed category, the respective calculated fee will be displayed on a dialog box when the user presses the "Calculate Fee" button.
